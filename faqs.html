Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is MEA?

MEA stands for Mechanical Engineering Association, IIT Bombay. It organizes events, workshops, and tech fests.

Q2: Who can join MEA?

All students from the Mechanical Engineering department can be a part of MEA activities.

Q3: How do I contact MEA?

You can reach out via the contact form on the Contact page or email us directly.
